About me

Crafting Innovative Solutions, One Line of Code at a Time

From a young age, I was fascinated by the world of technology. This passion led me to enroll in a Web Development Technical Course during high school at the Instituto Federal de Educação, Ciência e Tecnologia do Rio Grande do Sul. Eager to deepen my knowledge, I pursued a degree in System Analysis and Development, where I discovered a love for creating solutions that blend creativity with technical prowess.

My early career began with a focus on optimizing and automating processes. I thrived in environments where I could refactor complex systems to run faster and more efficiently, setting the foundation for my expertise in full-stack development. Over the years, I transitioned from creating dynamic dashboards and APIs to leading major software projects, always driven by a commitment to performance, scalability, and user-centric design.

I have consistently embraced challenges that allowed me to develop new features, optimize existing code, and ensure seamless integration across platforms. My journey has been marked by a relentless pursuit of learning and improvement, mentoring fellow developers, and contributing to impactful projects that enhance user experiences.

With a strong foundation in technologies such as NodeJS, React, TypeScript, AWS, and more, I am passionate about building innovative solutions that not only solve problems but also create value and drive growth. My story is one of continuous learning, a passion for development, and a commitment to excellence.
